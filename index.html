 Search

Cydog Browser has released a randomized search feature that lets you limit your meta data footprint by spreading your searches around to multiple providers. It also gains you better search results, as many search engines suffer from Search Engine Optimization (SEO) poisoning that manipulate what searches are attached to which queries.
